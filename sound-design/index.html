Sound Design

With credits such as Sound Designer, Dialogue Editor, Music Composer, Re-recording Mixer and Location Sound Recording on a number of short films and TV mini series. Lloyd has a history of experience in working with sound for video.

Lloyd has received awards for Purgatory (2010) being ‘Platinum Reel Award Winner’ at Nevada Film Festival and Nomination as ‘Best Sound for a Short Fiction Film’ by the Australian Screen Sound Guild for Crossroad (2014)
