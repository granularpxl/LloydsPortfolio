Artist Portfolio

Lloyd is an artist interested in the intersection of music, sound design, video and nature and how these elements can be arranged in interactive networks using technology. His multisensory artworks pull from a background as a classical and jazz pianist turned electronic music producer as well as from his passion for exploring natural environments and making field recordings. His works engage with composition through coding and logic systems, experimentation with synthesizers and sampling, taking form as audio visual performances, installation artworks and recorded sound.
