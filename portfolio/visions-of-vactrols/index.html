Visions of Vactrols

Visions of Vactrols was written late in 2013, when I was beginning to understand the limitless potential of sampling the world around me. Many of the tracks began as reincarnations of previous works, sampled and deformed to become something new, with the intention that they too would eventually be recycled. In contrast to my previous release “Elbee”, I was interested in using natural sound sources, incorporating stronger grooves and an experimental approach to composition and sound design.

Released in June 2014 on Wood and Wire / New Weird Australia
